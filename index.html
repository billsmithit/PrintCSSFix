The Fix

After some much needed research the PrintCSSFix helps to solve the headache that is making sure your site looks amazing when in print!. Not that having a responsive template is enough, now you can link printcssfix.css and all will be 'fixed'.

Take a look at what the fix does by print previewing the page (or print it if you want too!)

<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="https://s3-eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/printcssfix/printfix.css">

OR do the normal git thing:

$ https://github.com/billsmithit/PrintCSSFix.git

Feel free to add or tweet any further updates to @billsmithit

Designer Notes

No need to worry about the design, the PrintCSSFix examines your design, in detail. Then it wipes it clean and lays down up to 40 pages of pure, dark, sexy black ink. You can't get any more web 2.0 or flat then the PrintCSSFix
